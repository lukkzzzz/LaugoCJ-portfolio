Duyan

Rest House/ Retirement House

The retirement house/ rest house is at Ayala West Grove Heights, A residential development under Ayala Land in Silang, Cavite. The lot is accessible since it is adjacent to an easement with a view of Laguna de Bay and greeneries. It is a contemporary rest house with five bedrooms and a pool. It has two carport, one from the front and rear side.

Hestia’s Haven

Townhouse

The Hestia’s Haven is located in Heroes Hill in Sta. Cruz, Quezon City, which belongs to the Triangle and Scout area, wherein the area is called the Central Business District.

The project takes inspiration from the Goddess of Hearth, who is known for protecting family life and home. Hestia’s Haven is designed with warm colors and natural materials that create a cozy atmosphere and earthy interiors associated with Hestia. The ground floor has an open floor plan that promotes family bonding.

Prism “The Diamond of BGC”

Highrise mixed-use (Office Zone)

This project was designed and planned by eight people, aiming to build an iconic building in BGC. The floor plan and perspective of office zone were the only things included in the portfolio because those were the only aspects worked on by the designer.

This offices are small offices and medium offices with different concepts applied. Small office are designed traditionally, where employees work and leave the offices., on the other hand, medium offices are designed in hybrid, where there are different working station which employees are the one to decide where to sit and work. Medium offices have three zones; the social zone where employees collaborate with other teams or employees, the focused work where employees are focused on their work at the same time interact and collaborate with other people. This zone are in the middle where it is less noisy than the social zone. Lastly, the quiet zone where people are just focused on their work.
